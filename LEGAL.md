## 4. Clarification Regarding Automated Operations

Pursuant to Article 10 of the *Measures for the Administration of Internet Advertising* of the People's Republic of China, where an internet advertisement is published in the form of a pop-up or similar format, advertisers and advertising publishers shall prominently display a close button to ensure one-click closure, and the following situations are prohibited:

(1) The absence of a close button, or the advertisement being closeable only after a countdown has elapsed;

(2) The close button being fake, not clearly identifiable, or difficult to locate, thereby creating obstacles to closing the advertisement;

(3) Closing the advertisement requiring more than two clicks;

(4) While browsing the same page or document, the advertisement continuing to reappear after being closed, interfering with the user's normal use of the network;

(5) Any other conduct that impairs one-click closure.

The foregoing provisions apply to splash-screen advertisements displayed or published upon launching an internet application.

The rules in this project attempt automated clicks only where a close or skip control is present in the interface. Such automation constitutes the automated execution of a closure action already available to the user. The rules do not modify or replace advertising content, nor do they intervene in any advertising delivery system.

## 4. 关于自动化操作的相关说明

根据中华人民共和国《互联网广告管理办法》第十条规定：以弹出等形式发布互联网广告，广告主、广告发布者应当显著标明关闭标志，确保一键关闭，不得有下列情形：

（一）没有关闭标志或者计时结束才能关闭广告；

（二）关闭标志虚假、不可清晰辨识或者难以定位等，为关闭广告设置障碍；

（三）关闭广告须经两次以上点击；

（四）在浏览同一页面、同一文档过程中，关闭后继续弹出广告，影响用户正常使用网络；

（五）其他影响一键关闭的行为。

启动互联网应用程序时展示、发布的开屏广告适用前款规定。

本项目规则仅在界面存在关闭/跳过等控件时尝试执行自动化点击，属于对用户已拥有的关闭操作的自动化，并不修改或替换广告内容，也不对广告投放系统进行干预。
